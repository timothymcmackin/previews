val generator : burn_in:int -> Stdlib.Random.State.t -> michelson_data Stats.Gen.t

generator ~burn_in rng_state performs a burn-in phase consisting of sampling burn_in times, throwing the results away and returns a michelson term sampler. The goal of burn-in is to drive the underlying Markov chain to its stationary distribution, ie to sample terms around the specified X.target_size.
